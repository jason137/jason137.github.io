biology

An Introduction to Systems Biology: Design Principles of Biological Circuits, Alon (2006)

Introduction to Computational Genomics: A Case Studies Approach, Cristianini & Hahn (2006)

The Ideas of Biology, Bonner, JT (1962)
This is a well-written discussion of some of the big ideas in biology. Bonner outlines a rapidly-changing field at a stage in its development where big contemporary trends (eg genomics, systems biology) were still over the horizon, but scientists were beginning to suspect their existence. An interesting illustration of biological principles, and also of the way that science takes shape.
